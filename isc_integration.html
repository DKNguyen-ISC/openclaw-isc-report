Company-Wide Rollout Strategy

The 5W-2H Framework

5W-2H Strategy Execution

To systematically scale Kaizen and 5S principles, we apply the 5W-2H framework at three distinct levels of the organization: Individual, Department, and Company.

Level WHAT (Action) WHY (Benefit) WHEN (Timeline) WHO (Owner) WHERE (Platform) HOW TO (Method) HOW MUCH (Cost)
Cá nhân (Individual) Automate daily manual repetitive tasks Eliminate Waiting & Defects (7-Wastes) Immediate effect upon deployment Individual PICs Google Sheets / Excel macros Formula optimization, simple Apps Scripts $0 (Zero added cost)
Phòng ban (Department) E.g. Auto-calculate SCM Shortage (Demand - Stock + POs) Standardize processes, reduce bottlenecks and cross-communication Within Q1 - Q3 2026 Department Managers (e.g. SC Lead) Department Data Silos (BigQuery) Automated Scheduled Queries, Auto-Email alerts to Purchasing Minimal (~$10-15/mo for Queries)
Công ty (Company) Cross-departmental data synchronization (SCM ↔ Prod ↔ Finance) Achieve ultimate CEO visibility & comprehensive Digital Transformation End of 2026 Rollout CEO Kent / Tech Leads Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) Live Dashboards (Looker Studio), API integrations High ROI ($5k/yr infrastructure for $500k+ efficiency yield)

Standardized Approach

This identical framework is mandated for all new digitalization initiatives. By framing every project through 5W-2H, we ensure alignment with the overarching goal of reducing Muda (waste) across ISC.
